# Medicare-Plus-Hospital-Healthcare-performance-dashboard
MediCare Plus Hospital is a leading multi-specialty healthcare provider with clinics across multiple cities. The organization aims to deliver high-quality care while maintaining operational efficiency. With hundreds of patients visits every month, management wants to leverage data analytics to understand treatment patterns, cost variations, and doctor performance. By analyzing patient visit data, MediCare Plus seeks to improve diagnosis accuracy, reduce waiting times, and identify areas for cost optimization.
